About us

Medicure Pulse seeks to uplift medical Diagnostics to its highest level in the same manner that it seeks to preserve the patients dignity and respect especially in what could be one of the most difficult phases in their lives. We also seek to lighten the load in facing a possible medical condition primarily by ensuring that only reliable results are obtained from testing and the imaging procedures performed.

At Medicure Pulse, we consider it our mission to improve the lives of those we serve by providing only the highest quality care and Diagnostic imaging services.

  • The best of technology and know how


    • Committed to deliver world-class quality, Medicure Pulse Diagnostic Centre are equipped with state-of-the-art equipments. The technology is backed by high caliber diagnosticians: radiologists, bio-chemists, pathologists, microbiologists as well as skilled technicians. Diagnostic services range from Radiology to Pathology and Histopathology and Molecular Biology.

  • Setting benchmarks in service


    • Service has always been the distinguishing factor in Medicure Pulse's popularity. Whether it is the comfort, convenience or courtesy at the Centres or in delivering quick, accurate reports, we have led industry practices in India. Today with services such as online reports and SMS alerts we continue to lead the way in leveraging technology for better services.

    Medicure Pulse operates on three (3) main premises namely reliability, cost-efficiency, and rapid turn-around times for appointments and accurate results. The steps that make up the process of obtaining laboratory and imaging results have been made to comply with industry standards to ensure the highest level of quality and accuracy.


    The Medicure Pulse internal infrastructure widely supports the main goal of providing only the results that can be relied upon by medical professionals to confidently dispense their services and for patients to have peace of mind in having access to accurate findings. Laboratory and imaging results are of no use when presented in poor quality since accuracy of reading will remain an issue. Medicure Pulse does not waste the patients time, money, and effort by producing medical test results based on set industry standards.
