Pregnancy Scans:

Getting routine ultrasound scans during the pregnancy helps in monitoring the health and wellness of the fetus and baby. Also, it acts as a preventative measure to detect structural abnormalities in the fetus as well as monitoring the pregnant well-being of the mother.
